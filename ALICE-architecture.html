Analysis Engines

Gaze Deviation

MediaPipe + Custom LSTM

Tracks eye position vs. center frame. Classifies deviation patterns against per-contact baseline. Blink rate anomalies.

🔊

Wav2Vec 2.0 + Praat

Pitch (F0), jitter, shimmer, HNR extraction. Deep speech representation. Stress indicator detection vs. baseline.

📝

Contradiction NLP

DeBERTa-v3 + pgvector

Natural Language Inference on statements. Compares against per-contact history stored as embeddings. Semantic consistency scoring.

🗜️ Compression Pipeline COST OPT

▼
H.265/HEVC (on-device)Hardware MediaCodec encoding. 720p@24fps, CRF 28. 40-50% smaller than H.264. Sufficient for facial landmarks.
Opus (audio)32kbps mono. 95% compression vs raw PCM. Optimized for speech. Extracted server-side via FFmpeg.
Protobuf (landmarks)468 facial points per frame as protobuf. ~50MB per 30-min call vs ~2GB raw video. 97% reduction.
AV1 (server re-encode)SVT-AV1 slow preset. 20-30% smaller than HEVC. Done async. Used for long-term video storage.
AVIF (thumbnails)Preview frames in AVIF format. 50% smaller than WebP for equivalent quality.
